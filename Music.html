Three of My Favorite Bands

Nirvana

This band was made up of Kurt Cobain (vocals and lead guitarist), Krist Noveselic (bassist), Pat Smear (rhythm guitarist), and five different drummers most notably Dave Grohl. Nirvana went through multiple names for example there was Ted Ed Fred, Fecal Matter, Skid Row, etc. 2 Years After their formation they released their first album "Bleach", they were officially a part of the grunge scene after its release. But then their popularity increased dramatically when they released "Nevermind", this album included songs like Smells Like Teen Spirit, Breed and, Inbloom. this album hit number one on the billboard album charts. They then released their third album "In Utero" this album took a whole new sound, it included songs like Heart Shaped Box, Serve the Servants and, All Apologies. But sadly the band came to a halt when the lead singer commited suicide, this marked the end of Nirvana. All though the band has ended it still has a remaining impact on music nowadays.
